## 2.3 Controllers / endpoints ‚Äì rights usage inventory

This document corresponds to plan section **2.3** and todos:

- `inventory-controllers-statistics-audit`

The lists below are derived from controller usages of
`(new Helper\User($request ...))->checkRights(...)`,
`$workstation->checkRights(...)`, and related patterns, grouped by the
domains described in the plan. Only files under `zmsapi/src/Zmsapi/` are
listed here; helper and entity usages are covered in 2.1.
